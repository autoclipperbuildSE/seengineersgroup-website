Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. Overview

Auto Clipper is a desktop application that helps users create and manage short-form video content. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when users use Auto Clipper and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Auto Clipper may store user-provided account labels, content project information, captions, hashtags, upload status, and application settings. The desktop application is designed to store this information locally on the user's device unless a user chooses to connect an external platform.

3. TikTok Authorization Data

If a user connects a TikTok account, Auto Clipper may use TikTok authorization tokens only to perform actions authorized by the user, such as preparing or uploading user-selected video content. Tokens are used only for the connected user's account and requested publishing workflow.

4. How We Use Information

  • Generate and manage video clips selected by the user.
  • Store captions, hashtags, account labels, and upload queue status.
  • Enable user-authorized upload workflows.
  • Improve the reliability and usability of the application.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We do not share user information with third parties except when required to provide user-authorized integrations, comply with law, or protect the security of the service.

6. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the rights and permissions necessary to use, edit, upload, or publish any content processed through Auto Clipper.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on the user's device until deleted by the user. Platform authorization data may be removed by disconnecting the connected account or revoking access through the platform provider.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the information listed on the Contact page.
