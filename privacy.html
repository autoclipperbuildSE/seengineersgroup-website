Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 1, 2026

1. Overview

SE Engineers Group develops software tools including Auto Clipper, a desktop application for short-form content workflows. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when users use our software and related services.

2. Information We Collect

Auto Clipper may store user-provided account labels, project information, captions, hashtags, clip metadata, upload status and application settings. The application is designed to store operational data locally unless a user connects an external platform.

3. Platform Authorization Data

If a user connects a third-party platform such as TikTok, authorization tokens may be used only for user-authorized actions such as preparing or uploading user-selected content. Tokens are used only for the connected user's account and requested workflow.

4. How We Use Information

  • Generate and manage clips selected by the user.
  • Store captions, hashtags, account labels and upload queue status.
  • Enable user-authorized publishing workflows.
  • Improve application reliability and user experience.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties except when required for user-authorized integrations, legal compliance, security or service operation.

6. User Responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring they have all required rights, permissions and licenses for any content they upload, edit, process or publish using our tools.

7. Data Retention

Locally stored data remains on the user's device until deleted by the user. External platform access can be revoked through the relevant platform provider.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the information listed on our Contact page.
